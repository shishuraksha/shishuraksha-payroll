  • • Total: 29 + 3 + 0 = 32 days

✅ Fixed - Now Shows 32 Days!

With the hospital's 24-hour attendance rules, this employee gets paid for 32 days even though June only has 30 days. This is because:

  • • Each P+OT day counts as 2 days payment (regular + overtime)
  • • All 4 offs are paid (within the 4-off limit)
  • • No unused offs bonus since they took all 4 allowed offs
  • • Absent days are already excluded from base calculation
